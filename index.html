Game Description

Step into the boots of a pioneer and embark on an unforgettable journey in The Oregon Trail: Boom Town! Build your own thriving settlement, manage resources, and make critical survival decisions as you navigate the untamed frontier. Trade, farm, and explore to grow your town while facing the unpredictable challenges of pioneer life. Will you create the ultimate boom town and leave your mark on history?
