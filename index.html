About Game

RAINI: The Lords of Light is an epic fantasy card game where strategy, skill, and deck-building mastery determine the victors. FAQ

  • Is RAINI: The Lords of Light free to play?
    Yes! The game is free with optional in-app purchases.
  • Do I need an internet connection?
    Yes, online connectivity is required for PvP battles and events.
  • How do I get more powerful cards?
    Cards can be earned through battles, quests, seasonal events, and in-game rewards.
  • Can I trade cards with other players?
    Currently, trading is not available, but special events allow players to earn rare cards.
  • What platforms is the game available on?
    RAINI: The Lords of Light is available on Android, with an iOS version in development.
